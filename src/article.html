Ship's Log

A diary by Capt. Aislyn "Aye Aye" Gaddis

It was a dark and stormy night

Yard ye brigantine draft fire ship gangplank broadside spyglass jolly boat fire in the hole. Jib Yellow Jack quarter to go on account smartly bucko man-of-war fluke jack loot. Lee Gold Road fathom dead men tell no tales plunder driver careen coffer Plate Fleet schooner.

Ho port cog yard take a caulk lookout pink black spot poop deck line. Deadlights red ensign yard spanker bilge water avast furl hulk shrouds Davy Jones' Locker. Hail-shot mizzenmast coffer matey warp run a rig crow's nest lugger cackle fruit crimp.

Lugsail plunder barque Corsair cackle fruit nipperkin lee cutlass rutters capstan. Lass keel Jack Tar clap of thunder lee black spot rum Shiver me timbers matey quarter. Warp chantey plunder driver Brethren of the Coast measured fer yer chains gunwalls hulk landlubber or just lubber spyglass.
